## Things deliberately deferred

- **Retiree archive.** Retiring a player drops their `careerStats` and
  `careerAwards`. A future "retired-players archive" slice (probably alongside
  Hall-of-Fame mechanics) preserves this.
- **Playoff injury propagation.** `simulateSeason` only propagates injuries
  during regular season; playoff games don't update `Player.injury`.
- **Real free agency.** `advanceSeason` auto-renews expired contracts at the
  player's current tier. Replaced when Doc 7 ships.
- **Real NFL schedule formula.** Phase 2 uses random perfect matchings.
- **OL stats** (sacks allowed, pancakes) and **special-teams stats** — neither
  position group produces stat lines.
- **Coaching focus allocation.** Player Development doc's coaching-resource
  interface is skipped; every player gets baseline coaching attention.
