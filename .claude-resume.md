## What's parked on `wip/season-progression`

Two new files, typecheck passes, **no tests, not wired into the inspector**:

- `packages/engine/src/season/development.ts` — `advancePlayerDevelopment(prng, player, league)`:
  per-stage growth rates by skill category (physical/technical/mental/stable),
  archetype-modified (FAST_LEARNER, LATE_DEVELOPER, etc.), physical decline
  for veterans/aging, tier re-derivation from new skills.
- `packages/engine/src/season/advance.ts` — `advanceSeason(league)`:
  appends `TeamSeasonRecord` (with playoff outcome), recomputes
  competitive window from win pct, advances every player, decrements
  contracts (auto-renews expired ones at current tier as a Phase 2
  placeholder), increments `seasonNumber` + `tick`, clears schedule.

Both exported through `engine/season/index.ts` and the top-level
`@gmsim/engine` public API.
