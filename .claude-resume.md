## v0.13.0 highlights (this session)

Order in `advanceSeason`:
1. Decrement contracts
2. Retirement (active roster + off-roster pass for PS / unsigned FAs)
3. Activate IR → roster
4. `applyContractExpirations` → drops 0-yr contracts, players become FAs
5. `applyCapCuts` → over-cap teams cut for positive savings
6. `refillRosters` → tier-ordered FA market with multi-year deals
7. `refillPracticeSquad` → 16 fresh rookies per team

Order in `simulateSeason` per week:
1. Recover injuries
2. Play games (track `teamsDuringSeason`, `playersDuringSeason`,
   `contractsDuringSeason`)
3. Per-game injury propagation + IR moves for MAJOR
4. `runWeeklyPoaching` → fill gaps from PS pools (with 4-player
   protection per team)
5. `runWeeklyFreeAgentSignings` → fill remaining gaps from street FAs

Cap rule:
- Offseason phases: top-51 contracts count + dead money
- REGULAR_SEASON / PLAYOFFS: all 53 count + dead money
